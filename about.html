ABOUT OUR APP

Features

  • Add, delete, or cancel transactions anytime.
  • Record transactions as either income or expense.
  • Create and customize categories.
  • Monthly transaction summary showing total income, expenses, and balance.

Purpose

The main purpose of this web app is to help users become more aware of their financial habits and make smarter decisions. By keeping track of their money in an organized way, users can avoid overspending, plan ahead, and gradually achieve their financial goals.
